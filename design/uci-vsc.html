UC Irvine Veteran Services Center
Email, Social Media, and Print Design | September 2016 - June 2018

During my time as a Peer Advisor at the UC Irvine Veteran Services Center, I volunteered to create the Center's multimedia graphics for various events, such as new student orientations, panels, Veteran Appreciation Week events, and Memorial Week events, which were distributed throughout the UC Irvine campus in the form of mass distributed email announcements, Facebook posts, and printed flyers.
