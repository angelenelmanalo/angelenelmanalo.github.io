About Me

Hello! My name is Angelene Manalo, and I’m passionate about improving the lives of users and providing universal accessibility through design. I enjoy thinking about the big picture, creating a positive impact in all of my projects, and learning from those with different perspectives and backgrounds, so as to further improve my skills as an empathetic designer.

background

I'm currently studying User Experience Design at UC San Diego Extension, after having completed a Bachelor's in Computer Science at UC Irvine. I have experience in university student affairs, social media and email marketing, and brand, digital, print, web, and merchandise design.

values

I believe in the importance of social and environmental awareness, and the understanding that the products we create have a direct and lasting impact on not only individual users but on society and the environment as a whole. We have a responsibility to promote equality, diversity, and accessibility in everything that we do, all while keeping in mind the preservation of the planet. Through user-centric design, I strive to provide thoughtful and accessible products and services that can help to improve the lives of all users, especially those in marginalized communities. I'm particularly interested in intersectional feminism, fat acceptance, the LGBTQIA+ community, disability advocacy, and representation in media. My dream is to work in promoting and designing accessibility features in video games and other interactive media.

hobbies

In my spare time, I'm probably playing The Last of Us Part II, reading Descender, watching BNA, drawing, or taking photos of birds.
